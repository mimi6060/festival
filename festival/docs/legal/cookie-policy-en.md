## 1. What is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. It allows the site to remember certain information about your visit, such as your language preferences or login information.

### 1.1 Types of Tracking Technologies

We use different technologies:

- **HTTP Cookies:** Text files stored by the browser
- **Local Storage:** Persistent local storage
- **Session Storage:** Temporary local storage (session)
- **Pixels/Beacons:** Invisible images enabling tracking
- **Mobile SDK:** Development kits for the mobile application
